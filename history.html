Our History

Home history history page

Sports view schools was started in 2006 in a rental house. We enrolled three pupils that year. In the year 2016, we had our first Class Eight doing K.C.P.E National Examination. Since then, we have been doing the K.C.P.E National Examination in 2017, scored a mean score of 237.49, in 2018 we scored 240.58 and in 2019 we had a mean score of 243.43. We have qualified staff.

The school is a member of Kasarani Cluster. Quality and standard exams are set where pupils from member schools compete with each other.
